Who we are

Bunch of Hackz (BoH) was founded by Walter White, Jimmy McNulty, and Katheryn Hudson with the idea of drawing in people of all skills, whether it's programming, designing, engineering, etc., in order to create a positive environment that encourages collaboration. Since its inception, BoH has become a tight knit community that regularly meets together to express themselves creatively through projects. These events are referred to as a "hackerspace".


How do I join?

The Membership fee is $15 a month, and joining is as simple as sending us an email (see our Contact page for more info) or can be handled in person during one of our open nights.


Where we are

Each hackerspace and open night takes place at The Edge, Stanley Place, Cultural Centre, Southbank, Brisbane, QLD 4101. Sufficient parking is available; however the nearest bus stop, Racecourse Road, is located approximately 1500m away.
